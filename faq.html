FAQ - E-Portfolio

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is an e-portfolio?

An e-portfolio, short for electronic portfolio, is a digital collection of work samples, achievements, and experiences that showcase an individual's skills, knowledge, and accomplishments.

2. Why should I create an e-portfolio?

Creating an e-portfolio allows you to present your work and accomplishments in a professional and organized manner, making it easier for potential employers, clients, or academic institutions to evaluate your qualifications.

3. What should I include in my e-portfolio?

Your e-portfolio should include a variety of materials such as projects, presentations, artwork, writing samples, certificates, and any other relevant materials that demonstrate your skills and achievements.
